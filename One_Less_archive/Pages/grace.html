Understanding Grace

We hear and use the word GRACE all the time especially in scripture but I wonder how many of us really understand God's meaning of the word. If you hover over the underlined word, you will get a little pop-up of the meaning but trust me it goes much much deeper and I believe that in order to fully experience being a Christian, you must know what it means to live under your free gift of grace. The word is used countless times in the bible so clearly God means for us to understand exactly what it means and how it applies to your daily life. Below, I am going to give you some of the scriptures where the word is used which reflect the "ideal" understanding, then I will share the established thoughts and interpretations so that you can see the "real" application and how it fits into your walk with the Lord.

As a precursor, take a look at these 7 statements that I beleive establish the foundation of GRACE.

  1. Grace releases supernatural strength.
  2. Grace ignites a spirit of confidence in us.
  3. Grace SHOUTS that God is always with us as a friend and supporter.
  4. Grace points us to God who has a plan for your life.
  5. Grace reminds you that your outcome will be one of empathy, intamacy, confidence and faith.
  6. Grace assures us of God's sovereignty.
  7. Grace assures in us that God will turn things out for your good and his purpose.
